Shahi Paneer

Description

Shahi Paneer is a preparation of chhena or paneer, native to the Indian subcontinent, consisting of a thick gravy of cream, tomatoes and Indian spices.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • ¼ cup water
  • ½ pound paneer, cubed
  • 4 tomatoes, pureed
  • ½ teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • ½ teaspoon Kashmiri red chili powder
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • salt to taste
  • ¼ cup cream

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Cook onion and garlic until onions are soft and golden brown, about 5 minutes.
  3. Sprinkle cumin, coriander, turmeric, and chili powder over onion and garlic.
  4. Continue cooking until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  5. Add pureed tomatoes to the skillet. Cook until excess liquid evaporates and oil separates, 3 to 5 minutes.
  6. Add paneer, water, sugar, and salt. Stir gently so paneer does not break apart.
  7. Cook until paneer absorbs some liquid, about 10 minutes.
  8. Stir in cream and simmer another 5 minutes. Garnish with cilantro to serve.
